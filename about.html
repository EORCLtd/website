About EORC

Rigorous methodsfor uncertain futures.

Edinburgh Operational Research Corporation is a technology company specialising in optimisation and decision support under uncertainty for energy systems.

Who we are

A team focused on rigorous, scalable methods for planning and investment decisions in complex, uncertain environments — grounded in years of research and applied work.

Our focus

Operational research, applied mathematics and energy systems analysis — with explicit representations of uncertainty: demand evolution, technology costs, policy and market dynamics.

How we work

With asset owners, infrastructure planners and long-term system planners. We prioritise transparency, robustness and interpretability — so trade-offs and assumptions stay visible.
